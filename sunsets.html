    • SUNSETS IN PARADISE

    A Parisian Romance Set In The 1920's

    A few years after World War I, Alia Hart finds herself widowed and living an unsatisfactory life in Brooklyn with happiness hard to come by. Frank Horace, a surviving soldier from England, is living in Paris and struggling to find his place in the world after years of combat, anxiety, and trauma. Fate or coincidence brings Alia to Paris, where she meets Frank and in a series of surprising and unimaginable events, their lives become deeply entwined. Sunsets In Paradise is a tale of war, tragedy, and romance set in a 1920's Paris, where self-discovery and tempestuousness was the norm.
